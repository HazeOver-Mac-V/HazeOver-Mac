## Overview

HazeOver Professional Suite is a complete focus management platform with scheduling, presentation mode, and advanced display control for professionals who use focus enhancement as a core productivity strategy.

Scheduled dimming adjusts intensity based on time of day, applying lighter dimming during collaborative hours and stronger dimming during deep work periods. The schedule integrates with Focus modes for context-sensitive focus enhancement.

Presentation mode temporarily disables dimming or applies it selectively during screen sharing and presentations, preventing the dimming from affecting audience visibility. One-click activation handles the transition between focus mode and presentation mode.

Gesture and shortcut controls adjust dimming intensity on the fly using trackpad gestures or keyboard shortcuts, enabling quick adaptation to changing focus needs without opening settings.

HazeOver Professional Suite is the focus enhancement platform for professionals who use visual focus management as a daily productivity tool and need the scheduling, display control, and presentation features that professional workflows demand.

## Performance & Integration

HazeOver is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while HazeOver processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
